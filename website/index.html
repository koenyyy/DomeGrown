What It is

The Dome Grown garden is in essence an intelligent automated greenhouse in a geodesic shape, this is in order to optimize the use of sunlight. We at Dome Grown are as a startup aiming to promote and stimulate urban agriculture. The Dome Grown garden is an extension to modern day farming. All aspects of the Dome Grown garden will be designed to make the use of lighting, watering, feeding and housing as efficient as possible.

What we aim for

Growing food effortlessly, quickly and efficiently is what we aim to do. We'll provide you with an automated dome garden that takes care of your food while you are out saving the world.

    TU/e Contest

    In May 2016, Our founder Michael Verhagen contended in the TU/e Contest with his idea: GardenGarten. In the conceptual category he became the winner. After this he wanted to improve on his concepts and he started to compose a team.
