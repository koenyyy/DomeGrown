The idea

Simply put the Dome Grown garden is a smart greenhouse. The intelligent and automated greenhouse is built in a geodesic shape, this is in order to optimize the use of sunlight. We at Dome Grown are as a start-up aiming to promote and stimulate urban agriculture. The Dome Grown garden is an extension to modern day farming. All aspects of the Dome Grown garden will be designed to make the use of lighting, watering, feeding and housing as efficientand as possible.

What we aim for

Dome Grown is aiming to provide people with fresh and healthy food wherever they live. By combining different technologies, Dome Grown will grow your food in a healthy and efficient way. We aim to do so on rooftops and vacant lots in urbanized areas. The way your food is grown will be pesticide free and with the right amount of nutrients, resulting in a great pure tasting and healthy meal.
