Why?

We believe in changing the way we see our food production. The core of what we do aims at provoking a higher standard for your daily meal.

How?

We do this by making use of intelligent technologies, effective expertise and great modular design.

what?

We deliver urban farm units.

We provide spectrum wide consulting, innovative engineering & technology and connect customer-entrepreneurs to their markets via a software platform.

Control

The Dome Grown garden will be an automated closed system in which your food can enjoy a comfy life. The Dome will be equipped with systems that control the temperature, humidity, airflow, lighting and feeding. The controlled dome will take care of your food so you can do everything you want to do without having to maintain your produce.

Efficiency

Because a lot in your Dome Grown garden will be autonomous it will be possible to fine tune everything to perfection. By choosing the right materials, techniques and some smart programming it will be possible to make use of lighting, watering, and nutrients in a more efficient way. This will result in less power usage together with a higher and tastier produce yield.

Quality

Using the right materials is crucial. The Dome Grown garden will make use of durable components to build the dome to ensure it will be able to conquer the elements. Besides a structure of high quality, the plants will be grown in a nutrient rich, controlled environment which will ensure tastier foods. Due to the controlled environment, no more pesticides are needed.

  • Date: December 2016

The Panels in more detail

This is what the individual panels of the dome will look like.

Above you can see a more detailed view of one of the panels that will be used to construct the dome. The frame of the component will be made of aluminum. Aluminum was chosen for the great advantages it has to offer. Aluminum is corrosion resistant, which enables the dome to be more weather resistant and increase its lifetime. In addition to that, aluminum is very light weight. Due to the fact that it is light weight a strong structure can be built that doesn't weigh a lot. The center of the panel is made out of multiple layers of ETFE foil. This foil has very high insulating properties which can help reduce energy consumption for heating the dome.
